FUTURE PROOF,
HYBRID TECHNOLOGY.

BFIC has devised a hybrid new technology, capable to solve scalability and decentralized problems at all times, with maximum adoptability and flexibility.

Proof of Contribution (PoC)

An innovative blockchain paradigm that combines the advantages of Proof of Stake (PoS) and Practical Byzantine Fault Tolerance (PBFT) to constitute a new hybrid consensus algorithm.

Cross-Chain Communication

Fostering collaboration and compatibility between different blockchain networks and systems for exchange of data and assets.

Energy Efficient

BFIC blockchain consumes less energy as compare to Proof-of-Work and Proof-of-Stake protocols, while delivery a more advanced scale performance.

Increased Security

BFIC blockchain emphasizes security through advanced cryptographic mechanisms to ensure secure transaction verification processes.

Native Token

BFICoin, the native token of BFIC Blockchain, serves as the primary medium of exchange for all transactions and incentives within the BFIC ecosystem.
